Per Gessle Website

During a course in WordPress, me and my friend Theo were tasked with creating a website theme from scratch, no templates. We got in touch with the agent of a famous swedish artist called Per Gessle and began working together on a new design. We created the wireframes and mockups entirely on the online platform Figma.


The site was designed mobile first, as such it retains all of the functionality on both desktop and mobile. The site contains all of the information that the current website Gessle.com has - with the addition of some other functionalities such as filtering music.
