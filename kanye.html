Kanye Homepage Mockup

Decided to create a tribute to one of my favorite artists ever, Kanye West. These are four of my suggestions for how a Kanye West home page could look like. Each of the designs are built on the same wireframes - as such they feature the same logo, menu and external links.


The wireframes and mockups were mainly created in Adobe XD although Photoshop was used to create an image of Kanye with a transparent background. I've always been a sucker for teal, so that one had to be featured above the rest. Kanye if you're reading this, call me. Thanks.
